Overview

Cattaneo Grid (cattaneogrid.kevinroberts.ar) is a fully static web application. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about its visitors.

Data We Collect

None. This site has no registration, no login, no contact forms, no analytics scripts, and no advertising. We do not track your browsing behavior in any way.

Local Storage

The app saves your display preferences (theme and color mode) in your browser's localStorage. This data is stored entirely on your own device and is never transmitted anywhere. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

When you play an episode, audio is streamed directly from PodBean's content delivery network (mcdn.podbean.com). PodBean is an independent service with its own privacy policy. This site has no control over PodBean's data practices, and any data PodBean may collect is governed solely by their policies.

Cookies

This site does not set any cookies of its own. Any cookies set during audio playback originate from PodBean's CDN and are outside this site's control.

Children's Privacy

This site does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including minors.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated at any time without prior notice. The "last updated" date at the bottom of this page reflects the most current version.

Contact
